Best mud pie

This mud pie recipe is a dynamite version of a favorite dessert made by layering ice cream, chocolate fudge sauce, and cookies!

Ingrediends

    1 (6 ounce) chocolate sandwich cookie crumb crust (such as an OREO® crust) 1 (12 ounce) package miniature chocolate chips ½ (18 ounce) package crushed chocolate sandwich cookies, divided 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed ¾ cup chocolate syrup
